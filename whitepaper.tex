\section{Algorithmic Innovation: ``God Mode'' Math}
Central to Expenso's technical superiority is its integer-based \textbf{Graph-Theoretic Debt Minimization Engine} (internally coined ``God Mode''). Legacy systems rely on maintaining the exact state of historical pairwise edges, leading to massive redundant capital transfers. 

Let $G = (V,E)$ be a time-variant directed multigraph where $V$ represents user nodes and $E$ represents discrete debt vectors. Instead of natively resolving $E$, the engine independently computes the net continuous balance $B_i$ for every node $i \in V$:

\begin{equation}
B_i = \sum_{j \in V} w(j, i) - \sum_{j \in V} w(i, j)
\end{equation}

Where $w(u,v)$ is the magnitude of the expense paid by node $u$ benefiting node $v$. Currency is strictly parsed to integer minor units (Paise/Cents) to completely eradicate floating-point IEEE 754 precision loss. The nodes are partitioned into isolated sets of Creditors $C = \{i \mid B_i > 0\}$ and Debtors $D = \{j \mid B_j < 0\}$. The algorithm executes a greedy sequence of bipartite simplifications, matching $\max(B_c \in C)$ against $\min(B_d \in D)$.

\begin{equation}
\forall (c, d), \quad \text{Transfer } T = \min(|B_d|, B_c)
\end{equation}

By sequentially mutating the nodes and eliminating zeroed balances, the engine guarantees deterministic systemic settlement in a theoretical maximum of $|V| - 1$ transactions, optimizing routing efficiency from uncontrolled polynomial time down to $O(|V| \log |V|)$.

\section{Risk Mitigation Strategies}
\textbf{1. Non-Deterministic LLM Hallucinations:} Financial ledgers injected with non-deterministic LLM data invite risk of integer hallucinations or participant misidentification.
\textit{Mitigation:} Multi-shot prompt engineering strictly enforced via JSON schema constraints. Expenso rejects any NLP payload lacking a Boolean confidence flag and runs a deterministic physical computation to ensure $\sum \text{Split} \equiv \text{Total Amount} \pm 0.01$ prior to any Firestore I/O.

\textbf{2. Connection Desyncs (Network Partitioning):} Variable mobile network environments risk distributed ledger state collisions.
\textit{Mitigation:} Expenso leverages an asynchronous Offline-Sync CRDT pipeline. Telemetry operations are cached ephemerally and pushed up via native persistence queues. This ensures eventual consistency during connectivity loss, securing total data integrity.
